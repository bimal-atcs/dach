My Contraception - My Freedom

to be free and woman

The human body is a miracle of nature, especially when we think of the emergence of new life, of the nine months of pregnancy, when at the end you hold in your arms the result of the meeting of two cells.

Today, we women have the freedom to decide when we get pregnant personally or to consciously choose not to. The decision about the method of contraception is as individual as our personality and our life situation.

My freedom

It doesn't matter whether you have just met your first love, have moved into the same apartment with your partner, are a nursing mother or simply want to enjoy your sexual freedom in the middle of life - we offer you an overview of the most common contraceptive methods and all kinds of things worth knowing about the topic of sexuality.

For better readability and simplification of the language style, only one form of the gender is used here, namely the female. Men or people with a different gender identity are always included.
